    About Me

    Hi there! I’m Harriet Mugendi, a passionate front-end developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a keen eye for design and a love for technology, I specialize in building beautiful and user-friendly websites that deliver great experiences. I believe in the power of web development to transform ideas into reality, and I’m always excited about exploring new technologies and improving my craft.

    I have a background in law, but my love for coding has led me to pursue a career in tech. I’m constantly learning and growing, whether it's through personal projects or collaborating with other developers. I enjoy the challenge of turning complex problems into simple, intuitive solutions.

    When I’m not coding, you can find me watching anime, experimenting with new recipes, or enjoying a cup of chai. I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations, so feel free to reach out!
